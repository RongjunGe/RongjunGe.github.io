Biography

Dr. Rongjun Ge is currently an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Southeast University. His research focuses on medical image analysis, low-dose CT reconstruction and enhancement, foundation models for medical imaging, intelligent surgical systems, and human-centered artificial intelligence.

His recent work aims to develop reliable, generalizable, and clinically useful AI methods for medical imaging, including CT enhancement, multimodal representation learning, gaze-assisted medical image segmentation, and task-driven intelligent diagnosis.
